About Cebu.js

Cebu.js is organized to spread knowledge about the latest in javascript to developers in Cebu.

A community of sharing

New and exciting javascript technologies are surging. As developers we need to keep up with the latest technologies that are sprouting in our midst. We can't know it all but we can share what we know. So the floor is open for anyone who has something interesting to share. Contact us at talk@cebujs.com.

Speakers

Know of a slick javascript framework, interested to share to the community? Feel free to contact Cebujs at talk@cebujs.com.
